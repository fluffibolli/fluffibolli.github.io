How It Works

Our product is a sustainable toilet, mostly designed for countries hit by poverty (developing countries/Lower income countries). The idea is to give a clean and renewable toilets to satisfy all requirements. It will also keep all the citizens as clean and healthy as possible.

Our product is designed to be ergonomic and satisfy the user with the sufficient means to relieve themselves. It is a series of interconnected pipes and processes however the mechanism once explained is relatively simple.

The way our product works is that the user urinates through an elevated toilet seat then a sieve mechanism seperates the poo from the urine so that the urine descends down a tube and into a distillation pool.

This pool is shallow and above ground, thus allowing the sun's warmth to sufficiently heat up the raised plastic sheet placed here to collect the rising condensation from the urine below.

When the droplets of pure water have gathered on the sheet they will trickle down and fill a tube. This tube will be linked to a showering system, this will allow the inhabitants of the country to keep their bodies clean.

The reason we designed it so that the toilet and the showering system are conjoined is because is because the health of the person concerned stays considerably better due to easy access to highly sterile relieving systems.
